About Me

Full Stack Developer!

I am a software engineer with 8 years of experience in software development. Experience with Python, Django, Rest API, Unit testing, SQL, Full stack web development and refactoring. I also have knowledge and experience (1+ year) with PHP and Symfony, but I definitely prefer work with Python. Highly skilled in leadership, proactive, responsible and self-motivated. Along with these skills I worked as a Chief Technology Officer (CTO) in Colon's town hall. I am looking forward to bringing these skills to a new position as a full-stack developer.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of collaborating with diverse teams and clients, transforming ideas into functional, elegant, and scalable web applications. From platforms to content management systems, I've contributed to a wide array of projects, each one fueling my passion for innovation and excellence.

So, whether you're seeking a developer to breathe life into your web project, looking for a collaborator on your next venture, or simply curious to explore my work, I invite you to delve into my portfolio and discover the possibilities that Full Stack Development can offer.
